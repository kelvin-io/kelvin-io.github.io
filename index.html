F2 Geog Project Text from the Book (p.65,66)

Set up strict emission standards Many environmental problems are caused by the fuels used in vehicles. European emissions standards such as the Euro VI standard set limits for exhaust emissions of vehicles. The strict standards encourage car makers to design cars which are more fuel efficient and environmentally friendly.
Sign international agreements Only through international cooperation can the problem of global warming be eased. Therefore, many countries have been trying to reach an agreement to reduce the emission of greenhouse gases. At the Paris Climate Conference held in 2015, 195 countries accepted the agreement of legally-binding emission cuts. This agreement will enter into force in 2020 after the expiry of the Kyoto Protocol.
